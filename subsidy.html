PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

Get direct financial assistance from the Central Government of India.

Estimated Subsidy Table (2024)

System Capacity Central Subsidy Amount
1 kW System Up to ₹30,000
2 kW System Up to ₹60,000
3 kW to 10 kW Fixed ₹78,000

Eligibility: Only residential house owners are eligible. Commercial and Industrial buildings do not get direct subsidies but can claim Accelerated Depreciation.

Documents Needed

  • Latest Electricity Bill
  • Aadhar Card
  • Property Tax Receipt
  • Bank Passbook / Cancelled Cheque

Process

1. Site Survey
2. Feasibility via DISCOM
3. Installation
4. Net Metering
5. Subsidy Credit
